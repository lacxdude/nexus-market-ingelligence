# Nexus Market Intelligence (NMI)
**A Comprehensive Decision-Support Ecosystem**

NMI is an advanced analytical platform designed to provide traders with 360-degree market awareness. By unifying price action, macro data, and real-time news, NMI ensures that no critical information—from a major news break to a subtle BOS/CHoCH—goes unseen.

### Current Features in Development:
- **Structural Sentinel:** High-fidelity BOS/CHoCH and Supply/Demand visualization.
- **The Macro Guard:** Real-time monitoring of global data releases and news nukes.
- **Intelligence Hub:** Synthesis of technical and fundamental angles for high-confidence execution.
